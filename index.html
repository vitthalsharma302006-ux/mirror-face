⚠️ Important: What This Tool Does

Best for: Mirror selfies and mirror photos. These are naturally flipped, so this tool shows you the un-flipped version.

Not for: Regular camera photos taken with front-facing camera. Those are already the "real you" - no flip needed!

Pro Tip: Use your rear camera for the most accurate representation of how you actually look to others (no mirror involved).
